About Me

Hey, there! I'm Harjot Kaur Saini, a passionate creator specializing in web and mobile design. As a third-year student at Diablo Valley College, I am pursuing a degree in Interaction Design for Web and Mobile Platforms as well as Psychology, along with a Certificate in Business Marketing. My academic career is driven by a great passion about how design impacts human behavior, which I incorporate into every project I work on.

A hands-on approach to education has impacted my career path in design. Through a range of courses, I've been able to work on projects that reflect real-world issues, allowing me to combine academic understanding with practical application. This diverse experience not only improves my technical abilities, but it also broadens my creative approach, ensuring that each design is both visually appealing and intuitively useful.

On the technical side of things, I am deeply learning the fundamentals of current web development, beginning with HTML5 and CSS3, with hopes to soon incorporate JavaScript. Furthermore, my familiarity with industry-standard technologies such as Adobe Creative Suite and Semrush enables me to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also tactically successful. Looking ahead, I'm excited to get more expertise in the cosmetics industry, where I believe design has a transformational impact on brand identities and user experiences.
