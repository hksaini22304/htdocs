beautyGal - App Prototype

Category: UX/UI Design

Duration: 6 months

Preview

beautyGal is your go-to beauty empowerment app, created to transform the way you use cosmetics. The objective is simple: to promote self-confidence, creativity, and inclusion in the field of beauty.

My Approach

My goal was to empower individuals by providing a comprehensive beauty platform that fosters self-confidence, creativity, and inclusivity. beautyGal aims to inspire users to embrace beauty as a means of expression and self-assurance.

Vision and Innovation

My aim with this app was to empower people by offering a holistic beauty platform that promotes a sense of self-expression and confidence, while also hosting their makeup palettes.

Identifying Unique Challenges

Developing an interesting and truly user-focused platform in the cosmetics industry presented a unique challenge. beautyGal resolved the no-good beauty app conundrum.

Resolving Complex Problems

Navigating the beauty industry's digital world presented hurdles, ranging from technical advice to building an empowered community.

User-Centric Design

beautyGal is designed with users at its core, ensuring a seamless and engaging experience.

Meeting User Needs

beautyGal is crafted to address the demands of beauty enthusiasts who want individualized advice, sustainable product understanding, and a supportive community.

Detailed Pages and Features

  • Community Hub
  • Product Recommendations
  • Settings
  • Profile
  • Messages
  • Sustainability Center

Conclusion

beautyGal is more than just an app: it's a movement that reimagines beauty using customization, sustainability, and community.
